Our Story

A Taste of
Turkey in Batumi

Fatisa brings the warmth of Turkish hospitality to the heart of Batumi. Every dish is crafted from recipes passed down through generations — rich pides, silky menemen, and delicate baklava made fresh each morning.

Our kitchen uses only halal-certified ingredients, ensuring every guest dines with confidence and comfort. Whether you're stopping for a leisurely Turkish breakfast or an evening meal, we welcome you like family.
